About Chema Selecto Pork Skins

A Family Legacy of Quality Since 1967

Chema Selecto Pork Skins LLC is more than just a meat distributor—it’s a family tradition. Founded in 1967, our business has been built on a passion for delivering the finest pork and beef products to restaurants, taquerias, and meat markets nationwide. With decades of experience in the meat industry, we have perfected our craft, ensuring every cut of meat and every batch of chicharrones meets the highest standards of flavor, texture, and freshness. As a family-owned company, we take pride in passing down traditional recipes and techniques that make our products truly exceptional.

Whether you need crispy, handcrafted chicharrones, high-quality pork cuts for tacos, or premium beef selections for your restaurant, we provide fresh, reliable, and authentic products tailored to your business needs. Our commitment to quality extends beyond our meats—it’s in our dedication to customer satisfaction. We build lasting partnerships by offering exceptional service, competitive pricing, and a consistent supply of top-tier meats, ensuring that businesses of all sizes can depend on us.

When you choose Chema Selecto, you’re choosing more than just a supplier—you’re choosing a family that values tradition, integrity, and excellence. From small taquerias to large-scale meat markets, we are proud to serve communities across the country, bringing the authentic flavors of Mexico to every plate. Experience the Chema Selecto difference today.

Nationwide Meat Supplier for Taquerias & Meat Markets

As a national meat distributor, Chema Selecto delivers premium-quality pork and beef products to taquerias, restaurants, and meat markets across the country. Whether you need crispy chicharrones, tender diced pork for tacos, or traditional pork lard for cooking, we provide fresh, authentic, and consistently reliable products to support your business.

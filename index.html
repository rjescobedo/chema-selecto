Welcome to Chema Selecto Pork Skins

Authentic Pork Cracklings | Family-Owned Tradition | Unmatched Quality

At Chema Selecto, we specialize in crafting premium pork cracklings (chicharrones) using traditional recipes perfected over generations. As a family-owned business and a trusted national meat distributor, we take pride in delivering bold flavors, crispy textures, and the highest quality pork and beef products to customers across the country.

We provide fresh, authentic, and reliable products for taquerias, restaurants, and meat markets—whether you need crispy chicharrones for snacking, diced pork for tacos, or pork lard for traditional cooking. Our meticulous process and commitment to authenticity ensure an unforgettable taste in every bite.
